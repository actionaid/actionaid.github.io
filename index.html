Action for Global Justice

Strategy 2028
Change is possible, and it comes from the power in people.

It’s visible across the world; when voices join together, when one person inspires another, who inspires another. It’s more than just a dream, it’s an idea that works for those who need it.

Change is rooted in the individual and collective empowerment of those most affected to know, claim and defend their rights over the long term. That’s how we know that the grassroots programmes, the emergency relief, and the campaigning we do together can lead to a more just world.

We’ve been so inspired by the strength of people, often in the worst situations, to be the catalysts for meaningful change and justice. Often all it takes is the right kind of support. Here are some of their stories.
